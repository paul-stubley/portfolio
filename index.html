A little about me

I'm a Data and Decision Scientist, born in the UK, now living in Vancouver, BC.

Outside of work, I spend my time rock-climbing, skiing/boarding , baking and watching Netflix -
their use of data to develop shows they already know will be hits is visionary.

I've worked in both the consulting and start-up worlds, most recently as the Lead Data Scientist at Honeycomb.ai, an early-stage Vancouver-based start up enabling people with restricted diets to find food they can eat, anywhere. Before that, I was a Data Science manager for Deliveroo, a global food logistics & delivery company based in London.

I completed my PhD in Atomic & Laser Physics at the University of Oxford. My research involved using lasers to create, in the laboratory, conditions that only occur naturally at the centre of planets.
If you're destined to be one of the two people who read my thesis, you can find it here.
